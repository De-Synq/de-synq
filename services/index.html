We build backends in Typescript, either via AWS Lambda, Cloudflare workers, or NodeJS. We support backend applications with integrated dev ops. Depending on the scale and stage of the company, the solutions can vary greatly all the way from Next.js functions to Kubernetes. Our focus is on speed, and the integration of ops and development is key to unlocking fast and stable iterations that provide both platform stability and engineering productivity.
